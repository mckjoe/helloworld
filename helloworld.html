Hello World

A Program to Greet the Planet...

This page is an attempt to greet everybody on earth using our various human languages. We are interested to see how long the list is able to become.

Here are a couple of examples that I know off of the top of my head:

  • English: Hello, world!
  • Spanish: Hola, mundo!
  • Japanese: Konnichiwa, sekai!
  • French: Bonjour, munde!
  • Kinyarwanda: Mwirwe, isi!
